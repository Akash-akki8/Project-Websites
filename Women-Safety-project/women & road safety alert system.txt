introduction:-   

Women’s safety is a critical concern in today’s generation . Every day , we hear about incidents of harassment and unsafe travel conditions, making it essential to develop smart solutions that provide security and immediate assistance to a emergency contacts like police ambulance and parents . we using artificial intelligence and machine learning technology to this project.

problem statement:

1. Delayed emergency response – Help often arrives too late due to the lack of instant alerts.

2.Lack of real-time vehicle monitoring – There is no immediate system to track suspicious vehicle movements.

3. Insufficient safe route guidance – Many travelers are unaware of high-risk areas.

4. Limited vehicle communication – Vehicles do not exchange enough data to detect anomalies.

5. Underutilization of AI-based technologies – Many safety features are still manual and not automated.


Solutions: 

1. Incident Detection – The system uses AI and sensors to detect emergencies such as harassment, accidents, or suspicious behavior.
2. Vehicle Monitoring – It tracks vehicle speed, direction, and unusual activities to alert users of potential threats.
3. Safe Route Navigation – With the help of Google Maps, it suggests secure travel routes, avoiding unsafe areas.
4. Emergency Alerts – When an incident is detected, the system automatically sends alerts to emergency contacts and authorities with real-time location details.
5. Predictive Safety Analytics – The AI model continuously learns from incidents and improves its accuracy in detecting threats.


Methodology :

Data Collection: 
The system gathers data from sensors, audio recordings, and vehicle images.

Preprocessing: 
The collected data is cleaned, analyzed, and optimized for accurate results.

Model Training:
LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) is used for human activity recognition with 92.5% accuracy.
CNN (Convolutional Neural Network) classifies sound patterns related to incidents with 89.3% accuracy.
YOLO (You Only Look Once) detects vehicles and suspicious movements with 95.7% accuracy.

Integration:
The models are integrated into a user-friendly web application using Flask, Google Maps, and Twilio for alerts.

Deployment: 
The final system is designed to work in real-time, ensuring immediate responses to emergencies.



Results :

92.5% accuracy in human activity recognition
89.3% accuracy in sound classification
95.7% accuracy in vehicle detection
98% response rate for emergency alerts


Future Scope :
Improving AI models for better threat detection
Adding more safety features like facial recognition for identifying suspects
Expanding to more locations with improved real-time data analysis
